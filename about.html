About STRUKTURA

STRUKTURA Group provides demolition supervision, structural deconstruction coordination and industrial project support across heavy industrial environments.

Who Are We

STRUKTURA Group is built around controlled delivery, practical site experience and detailed planning. The company supports complex demolition and dismantling projects from early methodology development through to onsite execution.

With experience across industrial assets including silos, conveyors, gantries, load-out structures and structural steel infrastructure, STRUKTURA focuses on safe sequencing, clear communication and coordinated project delivery.

What We Do

Industrial Demolition

Controlled demolition planning and staged dismantling for industrial and infrastructure assets.

Lift Planning

Crane studies, lift sequencing, mass estimates and engineered lifting support.

Project Coordination

Site coordination, demolition documentation and practical project delivery support.
