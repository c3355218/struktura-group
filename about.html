Who We Are

STRUKTURA Group provides practical support across demolition, structural deconstruction, crane and lifting operations, industrial shutdowns and infrastructure works throughout New South Wales.

Built around real project experience and controlled delivery, STRUKTURA focuses on safe execution, detailed planning and practical coordination within active operational environments. The company supports projects from early-stage methodology and sequencing through to onsite supervision and delivery.

Experience includes work across bulk handling facilities, conveyors, gantries, transfer structures, silos, ROM systems, train load-out infrastructure, residential demolition and industrial steel structures.
