Project Overview

The train load-out facility formed part of the site’s material handling infrastructure. The project involved the safe dismantling and removal of the load-out bin, supporting steelwork and associated structures.

Detailed planning, lift studies and removal sequencing were undertaken to ensure the works were completed safely within a live industrial environment.

Key Features

  • Engineered lift studies
  • Structural sequencing
  • Dual crane lifts
  • Work pack preparation
  • Risk management and controls
  • Active industrial environment

Removal Methodology

1

Detailed site assessment and lift studies

Site inspections, 3D modelling and lift studies were completed to determine the safest removal methodology.

2

Sequenced dismantling

Top-down removal of the load-out bin and associated steelwork using staged dismantling techniques.

3

Controlled crane operations

Dual crane lifts were used to safely remove large structural sections within constrained areas.

4

Site clean-up and load out

All material was processed, sorted and removed from site in accordance with environmental requirements.
